Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries,and creamy brie. It will your new favorite holiday starter

Image Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce
Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500gr) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350 g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

for the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly slices
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubies (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cup fresh of frozen cranberries
Method
1.make the focaccia dough

In a medium sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dugh, tough I sometimes nead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
